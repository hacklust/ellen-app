A reliable friend

Ellen is a community-driven chat bot whom women can ask or talk to.

A fast learner

She learns from the interaction of the members of the online social community. Her knowledge is a collective representation of women's most common concerns so she gets smarter every time members interact.

A thoughtful community

Powering Ellen's knowledge base is an online social community where women can search for pieces of advice and helpful information to help make their life better.

Made with love

Ellen is created as an entry for Philippine Commission on Women's Mobile App Development Competition by Aaron Noel De Leon and Jose Marcelius Hipolito.
